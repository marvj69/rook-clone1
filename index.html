About Rook Game Scoring

How to Use: Tap a team to begin bidding, enter the bid and the points, then press "Submit." Keep track of all round scores in the “History” section. You can also freeze a current game, view saved games, start a new game, view statistics, toggle dark mode, and change the theme from the menu. Explore the statistics section to see overall wins, average bids, and more.

What's New in 1.2.7:

version 1.2.7 introduces a better way to handle team names and statistics. Here’s what’s new:

- Select teams: Team statistics save per team once team names are entered.
- Automatic Team Stats: Newly created team names are stored locally, along with wins, losses, and games played.
- Team Names in History & Freeze: The chosen team names appear in the “History” section and are preserved when you freeze a game.
- Expanded Statistics Section: View each team's record in "Team-Based Stats" with an ability to delete teams.
- Added separate team name fields for freezer games.
- Changed 'New Game' confirmation to a yes/no prompt.
- Various small UI and logic improvements.
