About Rook Game Scoring

How to Use: Tap a team to begin bidding, enter the bid and the points, then press "Submit." Keep track of all round scores in the “History” section. You can also freeze a current game, view saved games, or start a new game from the menu. Explore the statistics section to see overall wins, average bids, and more. Enjoy your Rook games!

What's New in 1.2.5:

Version 1.2.5 introduces a more dynamic way to handle team names and statistics, making your Rook experience even smoother. Here’s what’s new:

- Team Selection Modal on demand: No forced naming at the start.
- Automatic Team Stats: Newly created team names are stored locally, along with wins, losses, and games played.
- Team Names in History & Freeze: The chosen team names appear in the “History” section and are preserved when you freeze a game.
- Expanded Statistics Section: View each team's record in "Team-Based Stats" with an ability to delete teams.
- Added separate team name fields for freezer games.
- Changed 'New Game' confirmation to a yes/no prompt.
- Various small UI and logic improvements.
